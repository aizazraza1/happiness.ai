frequently asked questions

How is my privacy protected?

Your data is managed with the utmost confidentiality and security. You have complete control over when and with whom you share your emotions and information.

How does the app capture emotions?

The app uses advanced AI technologies to analyze and recognize human emotions in photos and videos. Since happiness ai is still experimental, emotion estimations may be inaccurate.

Why are negative emotions showing up too?

Just like in real life, where we experience a range of emotions, happiness ai detects a broad spectrum from negative to positive. You can use emotion filters to focus on the emotions that matter most to you.
