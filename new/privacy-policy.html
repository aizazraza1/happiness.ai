privacy policy

Happiness ai’s privacy policy describes how happiness ai collects, uses, and shares your personal information.

At happiness ai, we believe your privacy is key to your happiness and it is therefore one of your fundamental rights, wherever you live in the world.

collection of your personal information

We do not collect any personal information about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us. Certain happiness ai features may require you to be a registered user or provide personal information.

To better provide you with the happiness ai products and services we may collect and store some or all the following personally identifiable information you make available to us:

  • • information you provide when you contact us for help,
  • • information you provide when you register for a happiness ai account,
  • • information you provide when you use the happiness ai.

Happiness ai may collect and store additional information automatically, such as:

  • • device data (e.g., IP address, language preference, local time),
  • • information about how you use happiness ai (e.g., how many times you use the happiness ai each day),
  • • location data collected through your device (e.g., precise location data such as GPS).

use of your personal information

Happiness ai collects and uses your personal information to deliver products and services you have requested, to process your transactions, to communicate with you and to comply with law.

sharing information with third parties

Happiness ai does not sell, rent or lease its customer information to third parties. At happiness ai, we and our trusted service providers may use your personal information to operate and improve the happiness ai products and services, provide analytical data or to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, notices and marketing announcements.

links and social services

Happiness ai may utilize social networking or “share functionality” or may contain links to third-party sites and services that are not owned or controlled by the happiness ai. Please be aware that the happiness ai has no control over, and assumes no responsibility for, any share functionality or the content, privacy policies, or practices of such third-parties. We also may allow you to use third party sign in functionalities to leverage your existing third-party accounts to access features of the happiness ai. Please review your privacy settings on your third-party accounts, as those settings determine what information may be made available to us when you access the happiness ai via a third-party account.

use of cookies

The happiness ai websites, online services, interactive applications, email messages, and marketing materials may use “cookies” and other technologies such as pixel tags and web beacons to help you personalize your experience.

your privacy rights

At happiness ai, we respect your ability to know, access, correct, transfer, restrict the processing of, and delete your personal data. To exercise your privacy rights and choices, contact us via our help center.
Please note that in some situations we may not be able to delete your personal information – for example, when retaining your information is required by an existing legal obligation, to help ensure security and safety, to investigate deceptive, fraudulent or illegal activity, to enable solely internal uses of your information in a fully lawful manner.

children under thirteen

Happiness ai does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen. If you are under the age of thirteen, you must ask your parents or guardian for permission to use the happiness ai.
If a parent or legal guardian becomes aware that a child has provided us with information without their consent, the parent or legal guardian should contact us .

changes and contact

Happiness ai reserves the right, at its sole discretion, to modify or replace these terms of use, privacy policy from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to these terms of use, privacy policy by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account and/or by placing a prominent notice on our site and/or by posting the new version of our terms of use, privacy policy directly in our app.
Please consult this happiness ai terms of use, privacy policy regularly for any changes. Your continued use of happiness ai products and/or services after such modifications will constitute your: (a) acknowledgment of modified terms of use, privacy policy; and (b) agreement and consent to those terms of use, privacy policy. If you do not agree to the new terms of use, privacy policy you are no longer authorized to use the happiness ai.
Happiness ai welcomes your questions and comments regarding these terms of use, privacy policy please contact us via our help center.
